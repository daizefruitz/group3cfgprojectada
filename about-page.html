ABOUT US

FemTech is a charity supporting and uplifting women in the world of technology. Here to inspire other women to join the tech industry. Only 3% of women say that a career in technology is their first choice. 78% of women cannot name a famous woman working in technology. This is where we step in. We want to show people everyone the awe inspiring women working in technology today all over the world.

OUR BELIEFS

Courage

It takes courage to work in a male dominated field or to leave an existing career and start fresh, but we all have to take the first step.

Knowledge

Knowledge is the key to everything, we value learning, growing and then teaching those around you.

Diversity

To reflect the world around us everyone should get equal opportunities.
