ABOUT US

FemTech is a charity supporting and uplifting women in the world of technology. Here to inspire other women to join the tech industry. Only 3% of women say that a career in technology is their first choice. 78% of women cannot name a famous woman working in technology. This is where we step in. We want to show people everyone the awe inspiring women working in technology today all over the world. Welcome to FemTech, where we uplift women and champion their journey towards a fulfilling career in the ever-evolving world of technology. Our organization is dedicated to breaking barriers, promoting gender equality, and creating opportunities for women to thrive in the tech industry. We firmly believe that every woman possesses immense potential and deserves equal representation and opportunities in the tech sector. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and support women at all stages of their professional journey. We provide women with the tools, resources, and networks necessary to excel We offer a supportive community that fosters collaboration, mentorship, and personal growth.

OUR BELIEFS

Gender Equality in Tech

We firmly believe in the fundamental principle of gender equality. We believe that every woman should have an equal opportunity to pursue a career in technology, regardless of societal barriers or stereotypes. We advocate for inclusive practices, policies, and environments that foster a level playing field for women in the tech industry. By empowering women and encouraging their participation, we strive to create a more balanced and diverse tech community that harnesses the full potential of all individuals.

Empowerment through Education

We believe that education is the key to empowerment. We are committed to providing women with access to high-quality education, training, and resources in the field of technology. By equipping women with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need, we empower them to pursue their passion, overcome challenges, and succeed in their tech careers. We are dedicated to creating a supportive learning environment that nurtures women's growth and fosters their professional development.

Collaboration and Community

We believe in the power of collaboration and the strength of community. We recognize that creating change requires collective effort and the support of like-minded individuals. We encourage women in tech to come together, share experiences, and build strong networks of support. Through mentorship, networking events, and community outreach, we foster connections and create a sense of belonging for women in the tech industry. We believe that by working together, we can amplify our impact and inspire more women to pursue careers in technology.
